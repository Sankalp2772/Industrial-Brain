# ElectroDrive 500HP AC Induction Motor
**Model:** ED-500 (Asset: Motor M-22)
**Manufacturer:** ElectroDrive Systems

## Specifications
- **Voltage:** 4160V
- **Full Load Amps:** 62A
- **Max Operating Temp:** 85°C

## Protection
The motor is equipped with internal thermal overload protection. If the driven equipment (e.g. Pump) seizes or experiences high friction, the motor will draw excess current and trip the overload relay to prevent stator winding burnout.
