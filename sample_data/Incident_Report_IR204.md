# Incident Report: IR-204
**Date:** 2023-12-02
**Time:** 14:30
**Reporter:** Maria Gonzalez (Operations)

## Incident Description
At 14:30, Unit 4 experienced a sudden partial shutdown. 
The control room received a high-temperature alarm for Pump P-101, immediately followed by a motor overload trip on Motor M-22. 
Field operators reported a loud grinding noise and smoke coming from the outboard bearing housing of P-101.

## Immediate Actions Taken
- Locked out Motor M-22 at the MCC.
- Isolated Pump P-101 suction and discharge valves.
- Shifted cooling water load to the backup pump (P-102).

## Initial Damage Assessment
Pump P-101 shaft is completely seized. Motor M-22 smells of burnt insulation but stator windings might be intact.
