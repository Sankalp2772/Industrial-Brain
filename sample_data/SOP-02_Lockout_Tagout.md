# Standard Operating Procedure: SOP-02
**Title:** Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) Safety Protocol
**Last Updated:** 2023-01-15
**Author:** Safety Department

## 1. Scope
Mandatory for all mechanical and electrical maintenance on active machinery.

## 2. Requirements
- Engineer must identify all energy sources (Electrical, Pneumatic, Hydraulic).
- Apply personal lock to the primary breaker.
- Verify zero energy state before proceeding.
