# FlowMaster Industrial Centrifugal Pump Series
**Model:** FM-5000 (Asset: Pump P-101)
**Manufacturer:** FlowMaster Heavy Industries

## Section 4: Maintenance
The FM-5000 is equipped with high-speed ceramic bearings. 
**CRITICAL:** Bearings MUST be lubricated every 90 days (quarterly) with synthetic polyurea grease (SynthoLube-X9 or equivalent). Mixing grease types will cause sludge formation.

## Section 5: Troubleshooting
- **High Temperature / High Vibration:** Typically indicates bearing starvation or misalignment. 
- **Low Discharge Pressure:** Indicates impeller wear or cavitation.
