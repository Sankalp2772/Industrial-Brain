# Standard Operating Procedure: SOP-01
**Title:** Centrifugal Pump Maintenance Procedure
**Last Updated:** 2023-01-15
**Author:** Alex Chen, Reliability Engineer

## 1. Purpose
To establish standard maintenance practices for all centrifugal pumps across the facility, specifically targeting the P-100 series (including Pump P-101).

## 2. Schedule
- **Monthly:** Visual inspection for leaks.
- **Quarterly:** Bearing lubrication with SynthoLube-X9.
- **Bi-Annually:** Vibration analysis using handheld sensors.
- **Annually:** Full teardown and seal replacement.

## 3. Procedure for Lubrication
1. Ensure Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) per SOP-02.
2. Clean the zerk fittings on the bearing housing.
3. Apply exactly 15 grams of SynthoLube-X9.
4. Rotate shaft manually to distribute grease.
5. Record action in the maintenance log.

*Warning: Failure to lubricate quarterly can result in premature bearing failure and catastrophic seal damage.*
